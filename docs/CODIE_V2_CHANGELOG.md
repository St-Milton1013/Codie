# Codie V2 Constitutional Change Log

**Status:** Ratification record
**Date:** 2026-07-20
**Last amended:** 2026-07-21
**V1 reference:** `docs/CODIE_V1_CONSTITUTION.md`
**V2 authority:** `docs/CODIE_V2_CONSTITUTION.md`

## Purpose

This document records the approved differences between V1 and V2. It is a governance record, not implementation authorization.

## Product-boundary changes

- Jin-Gitaxias becomes a first-class, governed conversational strategy subsystem.
- Strategic reasoning is permitted only inside Jin's non-canonical boundary with evidence, confidence, speculation, contradiction, and legality disclosures.
- Theory Corpus, its attributed knowledge graph, a minimal Correction Ledger, judge-style education, Tournament Exposure Analysis, and Relationship Intelligence become core V2 capabilities.
- Codie remains local-first, evidence-first, zero-additional-cost, reproducible, and separate from Moxfield or other deck editors.

## Evidence and analytics changes

- Scryfall Tagger becomes scoped Class 0C authority for imported label identity and provenance, not functional completeness.
- Relationship Intelligence adds typed edges and measured support, directional confidence, dependence delta, lift, leverage, Jaccard, and PMI.
- Co-dependence remains metric-only and may not produce causal or strategic claims.
- Infinite or excess mana analysis must verify present conversion outlets and mana sinks.
- Tournament exposure begins with a labeled independent-seat approximation; Swiss modeling remains deferred.
- Unknown, unavailable, unsupported, not-applicable, insufficient-coverage, and legality-blocked states become distinct constitutional outcomes.
- Change Impact Analysis and provider health/freshness reporting become governed capabilities.

## Jin, theory, and corrections

- Jin may make bounded strategic arguments but may never mutate canonical evidence or persisted recommendation truth.
- Local model execution is the default and required fallback; cloud selection is stored per profile with data-class-specific disclosure and consent.
- Community context has no fixed recency window and never becomes measured evidence.
- Theory storage permits metadata, links, structured concepts, user notes, and short compliant excerpts, but not full mirrored works by default.
- Corrections gain scoped lifecycle states: proposed, verified, active, superseded, rejected, and revalidation-required.
- Jin releases require a fixed grounding, legality, scope-isolation, correction, privacy, and labeling regression corpus.

## Source changes

- cEDH.jp is audit-gated and becomes analytics-eligible only after acquisition, provenance, and deduplication validation.
- Lotus Refinery remains a research candidate until admitted through source review.
- Older theory sources remain eligible with date, format, and transferability caveats.
- Reddit RSS/Atom becomes an approved post-checkpoint Community Context discovery candidate for Jin, Theory Corpus candidates, research, rules review, tool evaluation, and experiments.
- Reddit RSS remains low-confidence by default and is prohibited from tournament injection, measured analytics, recommendation generation, canonical evidence writes, model training, bulk harvesting, or unattributed full-content storage.

## Simulation and rules changes

- Paradise Mantle, Springleaf Drum/Valley Floodcaller, target-turn semantics, retained-resource accounting, and trace-economy corrections become permanent regression requirements.
- Tutor piles use best-line analysis; opponent-dependent results must be labeled non-guaranteed and may not be called deterministic without branch-complete proof.
- A full multiplayer Magic rules engine remains outside core scope.

## Governance and V1 preservation

- Governed, tested, documented, recoverable migrations replace an absolute schema freeze.
- V1 remains unchanged as historical reference.
- Every V1 capability retains its V1 purpose, boundaries, and roadmap status except:
  1. the standalone Natural Language Query Helper is folded into Jin and the Scryfall/Tagger translation pipeline;
  2. mobile report delivery is deferred.
- The standalone Stream Deck Game Tracker remains removed; future Stream Deck controls remain deferred.

## Implementation consequence

V2 adoption changes constitutional authority only. Every runtime, schema, provider, analytics, UI, simulator, or model change still requires its normal contract and validation packet.
